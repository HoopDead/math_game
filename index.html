So... rules are pretty simple. You have to solve as many mathematic equations as you can in specific time. At start you'll get 15 seconds. For corret solution, you'll get +2 seconds to timer, and for wrong solution -2 seconds to timer. Of course, Enter button is indicated to submit solutions. That's all, good luck & have fun!
